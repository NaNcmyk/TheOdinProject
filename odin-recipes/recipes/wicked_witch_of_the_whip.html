Wicked Witch f the Whip

description

I'll get you, my pretty--and your corn chips, too! Guac-haha~

Recipe adapted from Fork and Beans.

ingredients

  • 2 large avocados
  • 1/4 red onion (thinly chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • juice of one lime
  • shredded carrots (for hair)
  • 2 green olive slices (for eyes)
  • 2 black olive slices (for eyebrows)
  • 2 slices mini red bell pepper (for lips)
  • 1 cornichon (for the nose)
  • handful blue corn chip (for the hat)
  • a dash of sea salt

steps

  1. Mash avocados in bowl. Add garlic, onions, and lime juice. Mix well. Season with sea salt for flavor.
  2. Add guacamole to lower half of serving plate.
  3. Create corn chip hat first, then add carrots for hair.
  4. Assemble face and serve!
