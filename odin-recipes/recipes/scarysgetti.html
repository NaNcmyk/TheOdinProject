Scarysgetti

description

Beware of pasta. Spaghetti may bite. Bon apÃ©tit.

Recipe adapted from Mouths of Mums.

ingredients

  • one handful spaghetti
  • 400ml tomato sauce
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves fresh garlic (crushed or finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • pitted black olives (for eyebrows, eyes, mustache, spider garnish)
  • mozzarella rounds (for eyes and fangs)
  • skins from a tomato (for eyeball blood vessels)
  • 1/2 cup soup stock (optional)
  • 1/4 cup fresh herbs (optional)
  • a pinch of salt & pepper (optional)

steps

  1. Add olive oil, salt, and pepper to non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and garlic. Gently fry until soft.
  3. Add herbs. Stir until well cooked.
  4. Add tomato sauce and soup stock. Simmer until sauce thickens.
  5. While pasta sauce is simmering, bring large saucepan of water to a boil and cook spaghetti till al dente.
  6. Arrange spaghetti on plates. Top with pasta sauce.
  7. Use mozarella, black olives, and tomato skins to create facial features.
