frankentast

description

Your toast...it's alive! It's ALIVE!

Recipe adapted from Brit + Co.

ingredients

  • 4 pieces of toast
  • 4 avocados
  • 1 tbs lemon juice
  • 2 sheets of seaweed
  • 1/2 red bell pepper
  • radishes
  • black olives
  • grape tomatoes
  • walnuts
  • mini dill pickles
  • a pinch of salt & pepper (optional)

steps

  1. Slice avocados in half. Scoop out avocado flesh into bowl. Add lemon juice and a pinch of salt and pepper. Then mash with fork.
  2. Prepare (and mix 'n' match) toppings for facial features of toast faces.
    • Use sharp scissors to cut out shapes from seaweed: circles for eyes, zigzags for hair, and thin strips for smiles, scars, and eyebrows.
    • Chop red bell pepper into small pieces to make lips, fangs, nostrils, and pupils.
    • Slice radishes for eyeballs and ears.
    • Slice olives into rings for eyes.
    • Walnuts can be used for ears.
    • Dill pickles can be used for noses.
  3. Spread mashed avocado on to toast and decorate. Let your imagination run wild!
